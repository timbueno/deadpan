Bound Privacy Policy

This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on Bound's app for iPhone and any other devices and platforms.

Information we collect

Bound does not transmit any identifying information to its servers (we do not even have servers to send information to).

All personal data downloaded with Bound is stored locally.

Bound does not and will never use cloud accounts for any reason except to the extent necessary to accomplish Bound’s functionality.

All data downloaded with Bound occurs from the results of the users actions.

Technical basics

We use tokens in the app to keep you logged into services you link.

Crash logs are collected in an anonymous fashion in order to improve the app.

Third Party Integration

The app does use third party services that may collect information used to identify you.

Link to privacy policy of third party service providers used by the app:

  • Dropbox
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Google Drive
  • Microsoft OneDrive
Analytics

Bound’s app collects aggregate, anonymous statistics, such as the percentage of users who use particular features, to improve the app.

Information usage

We use the information we collect to operate and improve our apps and customer support.

We do not share personal information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to accomplish Bound’s functionality.

We may disclose your information in response to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal requirements; to exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims; to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud or abuse, violations of our policies; or to protect our rights and property.

Security

We implement a variety of security measures to help keep your information secure. For instance, all communication with the app and website requires HTTPS communication. Passwords are never stored, and use login tokens store credentials between sessions.

Deleting information

Bound does not employ user accounts and collects only anonymous information.

Deleting Bound will stop anonymous information from being collected.

Bound may delete anonymous information at any time and for any reason, such as technical needs, legal concerns, abuse prevention, removal of idle accounts, data loss, or any other reason.

Third-party links and content

Bound downloads content from third-party cloud storage provider servers. These have their own independent privacy policies, and we have no responsibility or liability for their content or activities.

California Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

We comply with the California Online Privacy Protection Act. We therefore will not distribute your personal information to outside parties without your consent.

Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

We never collect or maintain information in our app from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our app is structured to attract anyone under 13.

Information for European Union Customers

By using Bound and providing your information, you authorize us to collect, use, and store your information outside of the European Union.

International Transfers of Information

Information may be processed, stored, and used outside of the country in which you are located. Data privacy laws vary across jurisdictions, and different laws may be applicable to your data depending on where it is processed, stored, or used.

Your Consent

By using our site or apps, you consent to our privacy policy.

Map memories, track adventures.

Embark on a new way to capture your outdoor adventures with Scoot, the privacy-focused journey tracking app. Whether you're cycling through city streets, jogging in the park, or taking a leisurely stroll, Scoot is your companion for recording and reliving your experiences.

Features

🚴 Track Your Journey

Precise, real-time route visualization and tracking for cycling, running, and walking.

🗺️ Relive Your Adventures

Interactive map of your routes and detailed statistics including speed and elevation.

📸 Remember The Moment

Photo capture and storage tied to specific points in your journey.

🔒 Save With Confidence

Private, secure storage of all your adventures.

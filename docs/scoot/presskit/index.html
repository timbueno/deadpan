What is Scoot?

Scoot is a privacy-focused journey tracking app designed for the casual exerciser and memory keeper. Whether you're cycling through city streets, jogging in the park, or taking a leisurely stroll, Scoot is your companion for recording and reliving your experiences.

Scoot is designed for casual exercisers and individuals who enjoy documenting their outdoor activities without the pressure of social sharing or performance comparisons.

Features and Pricing

Scoot is free to download with an optional Scoot Pro subscription. Scoot's premier feature is recording a user's journey for later reflection.

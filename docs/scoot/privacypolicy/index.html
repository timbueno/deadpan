Privacy Policy

Scoot takes your privacy very seriously. If you have opted into allowing Apple to share information with developers, we receive some anonymous analytics.

Personal Data

Scoot does not sell or rent your data, and any anonymous information collected is used only for improving the app.

Scoot also integrates the RevenueCat SDK which captures some analytics related to in-app purchases and subscriptions. You can find RevenueCat's privacy policy here. Scoot does not store any identifying information or the textual contents of your documents on any server.

Debugging Data

Scoot uses Apple’s opt-in service for crash reporting. Anonymous information such as the device type, date of the crash, and version of the operating system will be viewable to us. No personally identifiable information is sent or stored.

Any Questions?

If you have any questions about your data, please to not hesitate to contact me at feedback@deadpan.io.

Privacy Policy

Ephemera takes your privacy very seriously. Ephemera uses no third party frameworks or analytics tools of any kind.

Personal Data

No bookmark or any associated metadata is ever viewable or transmitted to us. All data is written directly to your device and any synced data is done so via Apple’s secure iCloud service and is not accessible to us. For debugging purposes, we may ask you to provide this kind of information to troubleshoot an issue. You are under no obligation to do so.

Debugging Data

Ephemera uses Apple’s opt-in service for crash reporting. Anonymous information such as the device type, date of the crash, and version of the operating system will be viewable to us. No personally identifiable information is sent or stored. The bookmarks you store, including all associated metadata such as the title, url, and the dates at which the bookmark was read or expired are not included in any crash report.

Any Questions?

If you have any questions about your data, please to not hesitate to contact me at feedback@deadpan.io.

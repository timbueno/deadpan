Read your bookmarks before they disappear.

Unlike other apps that save bookmarks that stay unread forever, Ephemera sets a deadline that the bookmark must be read by. Miss the deadline, and that bookmark is gone. It wasn't important anyway, right?

Features

Peace Of Mind

Never again drown under the weight of of unread, stale, “read later” bookmarks.

Configurable

Set a default expiration date that best aligns with your reading habits.

Notifications

Recieve notifications and always be warned before a bookmark expires.

Sync

Sync unread and read bookmarks across iPhone and iPad.

Statistics

See how many bookmarks you have read and how often bookmarks go unread.

Widgets

See unread bookmarks on your home screen so you never forget.
